BB Counter

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

BB Counter is designed to work without creating an account. We aim to minimize data collection and keep your information on your device.

Information We Process

  • On-device app data: chip values, blind settings, timer preferences, and related app state used to provide core features.
  • Support emails you send us: if you contact us, we process the information you voluntarily include in your email.

How Data Is Used

  • To provide BB calculation, blind timer, blind structure, and Live Activity features.
  • To respond to support requests, troubleshoot issues, and improve app reliability.

Data Sharing

BB Counter does not include functionality to upload your in-app chip/blind/timer data to a developer-operated server. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

App settings and values remain on your device unless you reset or remove the app. Emails sent to support may be retained as needed to handle your request.

Your Choices

  • You can reset app data from within the app.
  • You can disable Live Activities in iOS Settings.
  • You can contact us to request help with support-related data you submitted by email.

Children's Privacy

BB Counter is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
