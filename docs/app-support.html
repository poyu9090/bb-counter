繁體中文

這是什麼 App？

BB Counter 協助你在現場牌局或錦標賽中，依籌碼總量與目前大盲快速換算你還有多少 BB，並可搭配盲注計時與盲注結構；計時進行時可透過即時動態（Live Activity）在鎖屏與動態島查看重點資訊。

怎麼用（簡要）

  • 輸入籌碼 → 設定目前盲注（大盲）→ 在結果頁查看 BB 換算。
  • 可從結果頁編輯籌碼或盲注；支援快捷加減籌碼。
  • 開啟盲注計時後，請在系統設定中允許「即時動態」，以便在鎖屏／動態島顯示倒數與下一級盲注。

隱私

BB Counter 設計為無需註冊帳號。你輸入的籌碼、盲注與計時相關設定主要儲存在你的裝置上；本 App 並未內建將這些資料上傳至開發者伺服器的功能。若你透過電子郵件聯絡我們，僅會處理你信中自願提供的內容。

English

What is BB Counter?

BB Counter helps you estimate your stack in big blinds (BB) from your chip count and the current big blind, with an optional blind timer and blind structure. While the timer runs, Live Activities can show key info on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

Quick start

  • Enter chips → set the current blind level (big blind) → review your BB on the results screen.
  • Edit chips or blinds from the results screen; quick +/- chip buttons are available.
  • For Live Activities, allow the permission in Settings so countdown and next blind info can appear on the Lock Screen / Dynamic Island.

Privacy

BB Counter is built to work without a sign-in. Your inputs and related settings are stored on-device; the app does not include functionality to upload that data to a developer-operated server. If you email us, we only process what you voluntarily include in your message.
